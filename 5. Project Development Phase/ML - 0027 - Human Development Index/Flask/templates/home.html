Human Development Index

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a summary measure of average achievement in key dimensions of human development: a long and healthy life, being knowledgeable, and having a decent standard of living.

It is calculated using indicators such as life expectancy, mean years of schooling, expected years of schooling, and Gross National Income (GNI) per capita — combining these into a single score between 0 and 1.

This application uses a trained Machine Learning model (Linear Regression) to predict the HDI score of a country based on its key development indicators. Click Predict above to try it yourself.
